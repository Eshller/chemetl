ChemE Tinkerer's Lab

The Chemical Engineering Tinkerers' Lab is a one-of-a-kind makerspace for everyone in IIT Bombay to explore the diverse field of chemical engineering.
Ideated and built by our generous alumni of the 1975 batch , we're the latest addition to the Institute Technical Council, and work under the expert guidance of Prof. Venkat Gundabala. Being one of the few labs in the country that cater to solving chemical engineering problems, We aim to bridge the gap between industry and academia, cutting-edge research and hobby project

What does that mean? Well, it means you can work on everything from optimising existing processes to innovating something entirely new -- we'll be there to help you along the way. Looking to 3D-print some hardware so you can demonstrate a model of your thesis project? Or maybe you'd like to simulate how microfluidics provides insights into arterial blood flow? We're not just for chemical engineers -- if you love applying scientific principles, if you're craving a peer group as crazy as you, or even if you're just looking for a fun conversation, ChemE TL is the place for you.

Did we mention we are entirely student-run? That means we thrive on coffee and late nights -- we're open 24/7, step in whenever you like. We've got you covered.
